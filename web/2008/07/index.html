"Keep Me In Mind"
Little Joy
by Little Joy

Another side project... This time it's Strokes drummer Fabrizo Moretti. You can hear the Strokes in this song. I discovered these guys on Rhapsody when it recommended the album to me based on my ratings of other songs. I can't say enough good things about Rhapsody. Their recommendation engine has been one of the best I've seen (puts the iTunes Genious to shame). I hope they can continue to make it. I'd be very sad to lose that service. This is the first album by Little Joy. For some reason, the lead singer reminds me a bit of one of the Brat Pack. I found the entire album quite enjoyable. If you like this one you should definitely check it out.

2008
